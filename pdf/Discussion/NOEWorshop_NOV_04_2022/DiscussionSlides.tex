\begin{frame}{Summary}
\label{slide:Summary}

FinTech lenders are \alert{not discriminating} Black \& Hispanic borrowers after controlling for similar characteristics using \alert{matching analysis}, and may \alert{change discriminating behavior by traditional lenders}.

\vspace{1em}

Data used:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA) public dynamic loan dataset
    \item Optimal Blue data (OB) $ \Rightarrow  $ \underline{credit score} [\alert{proprietary}]
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{1em}

Contribution: compare w/  Bartlett, Morse, Stanton, and Wallace (2019),
\begin{enumerate}
    \item HMDA-OB $ \Rightarrow  $ control for \alert{upfront mortgage cost}
    \item HMDA-OB $ \Rightarrow  $ \alert{more precise} def of discrimination
    \item matching analysis $ \Rightarrow  $ control for zip-code level characteristics
\end{enumerate}
\end{frame}
